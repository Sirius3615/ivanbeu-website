01

Smart enough, without feeling complicated.

The useful promise of a smart home often gets buried beneath setup friction, incompatible products and interfaces that demand too much attention.

Wawe approaches the problem as one connected system. Modular smart switches are designed to fit different interiors, door and window sensors use detachable rechargeable batteries, and the mobile app brings device control, schedules, timers, away mode, shortcuts and automations into a consistent experience.

Electronics

Backend

02

One product, four coordinated layers.

Each layer is designed as part of the same experience. A physical action needs to arrive in the app clearly; an app command needs to reach the correct device reliably; automation rules need useful states from sensors.

03

Design decisions that reduce friction.

Modularity

Fit the home

Different finishes and a modular system let the product belong in an interior instead of reading as a gadget added later.

Autonomy

Useful on its own

Devices are conceived to operate independently, keeping the core interaction direct.

Automation

Start from intent

Shortcuts and sensor triggers bring relevant devices and current states together for faster setup.

Energy

Recharge, don’t discard

Sensor concepts use detachable rechargeable batteries and target long intervals between charges.
