Published on 2021-2022 • News Platform

Project Overview

Beyond Space News was a comprehensive space news platform delivering curated content across multiple channels.

Technical Stack

  • Flutter for mobile application
  • Node.js backend services
  • Social media integration

Platform Features

  • Real-time space news updates
  • Cross-platform content delivery
  • Rich media integration
  • Social media engagement
