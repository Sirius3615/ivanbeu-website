About Me

A little background on who I am and what I do.

I'm a developer and electronics enthusiast from Croatia, with expertise in building applications using Flutter, Nuxt.js, Vue, Node.js, and JavaScript. I also enjoy working on hardware projects, leveraging ESP32 and Arduino to create integrated IoT solutions. Skilled in cloud services like Firebase and Appwrite, I focus on innovation from app development to PCB design.

I'm passionate about electric vehicles, autonomous driving, and future technologies. Outside of tech, I love traveling, exploring diverse cuisines, and have a soft spot for cats. My optimism drives my curiosity and passion for creating.
